Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 16, 2025

BreakCycle respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data. BreakCycle processes everything on-device:

  • Preferences & Settings: Timer durations, working hours, and notification sounds are stored locally on your device.
  • Notifications: All notifications are scheduled and delivered locally on your device.
  • iCloud Sync (Optional): Settings may sync between your devices via Apple's iCloud Key-Value Store.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Send break and focus reminders if timers are running
  • Sync settings across your devices (optional, via iCloud)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information. No data is sent to external servers. iCloud sync is handled by Apple's infrastructure and is not accessible to the developer.

4. Security

Your data remains on your devices and, if enabled, in your personal iCloud account. We recommend keeping your operating system up to date and using a strong device passcode.

5. Your Choices

  • Disable timers in app settings
  • Disable iCloud sync in system settings
